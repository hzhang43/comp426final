How To Play


Controls:

Move using the left and right arrow keys



Objective:

Catch as many of the falling fruits as you can! Each fruit caught will add 1 to your total score.



Be careful! Some evildoer who hates fruit is also dropping bombs! Catching a bomb will cause you to lose a life



You only have 3 lives and if you reach 0 lives, you lose! Good luck!
